About

Undying Darkness is a Vienna-based melodic black metal inspired by the second wave of black metal. While remaining true to these influences, we push the boundaries of the genre with captivating orchestral passages, innovative song writing and unusual variety. Our debut The Eternal Abyss is a self-produced concept album that delves into the multifaceted nature of Death, exploring it from diverse musical and lyrical perspectives, while retaining a distinct and consistent sound.
